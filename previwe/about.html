About Our Products

Product Description:

• Mud and Mirror Work (also known as Lipan Kaam) is a traditional mural craft of Kutch, Gujarat, India. 'Lippan' means to apply. It is handcrafted on designer wooden pieces using clay, paint and mirrors etc.

• Lippan Art is quite useful for gifting, adds beauty and elegance to interior and exterior walls of home, offices etc. • It can also be used at the background of home temple and as Rangoli/ Diya Pallets during festivals.

• Every Lippan Art Work is made on 8 mm MDF water resistant sheet. It is durable, easy to clean and maintain. Every piece of more than 6" has hook(s) at the backside.

• Customized orders are also accepted and art pieces are made as per customer's selection of design, shape and colors.Our USP is to create and deliver big wall art pieces having area of more than 20 square feet. Our art pieces are reasonably priced, high in quality and are durable.

• Mud mirror work is nowadays gathering attention of the people for its intricate pattern and aesthetic perfection and has made a full transition from its unknown modest stature to the mainstream art world.

• We have more than 20 product varieties which include Wall Hangings, Name plates, Rangoli, Toran, Mandana designs, Shubh Labh to name a few.
