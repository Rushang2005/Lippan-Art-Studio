Our Brief Introduction

Hi There,

Lippan Art Studio is based in Jalgaon, Maharashtra. The Artist Mrs. Payal Bansal has a knack for creativity and she is an expert in mud mirror (known as lippan) art. This art is basically originated in Kutch (Gujarat).

The journey of Lippan Art Studio has been fun and full of excitement. We initially started this art with white color as a base and in standard shapes. Later on, our product range expanded to colorful designs and customized shapes of wheel, lotus, leaves, rose etc. We have also exhibited creativity in name plates, torans, door hangings among others!! We have successfully created and delivered big wall art pieces having area of more than 20 square feet.
